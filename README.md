# De-File
Game Jam project where you are an intern redactor for the world government. Your job is to guide a reader's eyes smoothly through a text (level) without them noticing sensitive information  by covering up words in redactable text sections  and create essentially the course the eyes will fall through while getting to the bottom of the page.


You are an intern redactor for a corrupt world government. You are tasked to mask information from the general audience by directing awareness of various pieces of media away from sensitive information by redacting, slashing and/ or drawing to guide eye flow away from things the government does not want the world to see. How long will you be able to mask all this sensitive information while keeping this mask on yourself?
Information, or rather the masking of it, is what controls narratives in this world. Who breaks first under the world's superpowers, the citizens or you…?
